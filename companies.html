Case Connect LLC

CEO & Co-Founder • Flagship Company

Case Connect is a leading legal lead generation company that delivers pre-signed personal injury cases to attorneys nationwide. The company specializes in motor vehicle accident (MVA) cases but has expanded into Lemon Law, Social Security Disability, and other legal verticals.

What sets Case Connect apart is its technology-forward approach. The company leverages AI-powered call analysis systems, sophisticated CRM integrations, and data-driven marketing optimization to achieve industry-leading conversion rates while maintaining strict compliance with advertising regulations including California's SB37.
